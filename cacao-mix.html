Ingredients

Raw cacao paste, unrefined coconut oil, ceremonial grade matcha, Dead Sea salt, lion's mane (fruiting body), Cordyceps militaris (fruiting body), Ceylon cinnamon, sunflower lecithin, organic ginger powder, turmeric powder (Lakadong), Rhodiola rosea extract, green cardamom, CoffeeBerry extract, ginseng extract (10% ginsenosides), Aframomum melegueta (Grains of Paradise), ginger CO₂ extract, clove CO₂ extract, star anise CO₂ extract.

Benefits

Raw Cacao Paste (6 g)
Rich in flavonoids (catechins and epicatechins) that improve blood flow, supporting cardiovascular and cognitive function. Contains theobromine (~84 mg) for gentle stimulation, mood enhancement, and vasodilation. Phenylethylamine (~35 mg) and anandamide (~140 μg) contribute to mood elevation and neurotransmitter balance. Includes theophylline (~1.4 mg), which aids respiratory and smooth muscle relaxation.
Unrefined Coconut Oil (5 g)
Provides medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) that support ketone production and rapid energy. The fat content optimizes absorption of curcuminoids and other fat-soluble bioactive compounds in the formula, promoting staged, prolonged release.
Dead Sea Salt (2 g)
Supplies a well-rounded electrolyte profile, to maintain hydration, nerve signaling, and muscle function.
Lion's Mane (1 g, fruiting body)
Contains hericenones that promote nerve growth factor (NGF) synthesis, supporting neuronal health, memory, and cognitive performance. Beta-glucans (≥25%) provide immune-modulating benefits.
Cordyceps militaris (1 g, fruiting body)
C. militaris contains significantly higher levels of cordycepin and adenosine than C. sinensis. These compounds enhance cAMP signaling and ATP production for cellular energy metabolism and oxygen utilization. The polysaccharides provide additional adaptogenic benefits, supporting stress resilience and immune function.
Organic Ginger (600 mg powder + 3 mg CO₂ extract)
Improves blood-brain barrier (BBB) function, stimulates mitophagy, enhances circulation, protein absorption, and gastrointestinal function. Contains shogaols and zingerone with neuroprotective and anti-inflammatory effects. The CO₂ extract provides concentrated bioactives without added bulk.
Ceylon Cinnamon (1 g)
Exhibits anti-hyperglycemic effects, improves blood glucose levels, and reduces inflammation. Lacks coumarin, a hepatotoxin found in other cinnamon types.
Sunflower Lecithin (600 mg)
Emulsifier that improves dispersion and bioavailability of fat-soluble compounds. Rich in phosphatidylcholine (an acetylcholine precursor supporting memory and focus) and phosphatidylserine (with documented cognitive benefits). Soy-free and non-GMO.
Ceremonial Grade Matcha (3 g)
Provides L-theanine for calm focus, EGCG for antioxidant and neuroprotective effects, and lutein and vitamin K for cognitive and metabolic support. Contains caffeine (~75 mg) and theophylline (~12.6 mg) for energy without crashes. Its polyphenols improve gut microbiota and metabolic markers, contributing to weight regulation and cognitive enhancement.
Turmeric Powder (300 mg, Lakadong 7% curcumin)
Lakadong turmeric from Meghalaya contains approximately 7% curcumin—several times higher than typical turmeric. Provides potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects supporting joint health and cellular protection. The lower dose compensates for higher potency and strong flavor.
Rhodiola Rosea (250 mg)
Adaptogen that reduces stress, enhances mental clarity, and improves energy regulation through salidroside and rosavins.
Green Cardamom (250 mg)
Facilitates digestion and provides antioxidants to support metabolic and gut health.
Clove CO₂ Extract (0.2–0.5 mg) & Star Anise CO₂ Extract (0.3–0.8 mg)
Concentrated aromatic extracts providing eugenol (clove) and anethole (star anise) for antimicrobial, antioxidant, and flavor-enhancing effects without the bulk of powdered spices.
Ginseng Extract (200 mg, 10% Ginsenosides)
Supports physical vitality and cognitive acuity via standardized ginsenosides, which regulate energy and stress.
CoffeeBerry Extract (200 mg)
Provides polyphenols that boost antioxidant capacity and cognitive health while contributing ~10 mg caffeine for gentle stimulation.

Stimulants

This blend offers naturally occurring stimulants from cacao, matcha, and CoffeeBerry. These ingredients provide cognitive and metabolic benefits with antioxidant effects and without the jitteriness or energy crashes typically associated with pure caffeine. The combined stimulants are balanced by adaptogenic herbs and beta-glucans.

Cacao contains theobromine, phenylethylamine (PEA), and anandamide, which collectively improve mood, focus, and neurotransmitter balance. Cacao's catechins and epicatechins help prolong the effects of these bioactive compounds while offering antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Matcha is rich in polyphenols like epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), along with L-theanine, which synergistically enhance cognitive focus, mitigates oxidative stress, and reduces inflammation. The antioxidants in matcha also protect against neurodegeneration and support metabolic health.

CoffeeBerry provides a gentle caffeine boost complemented by its polyphenols, which enhance cognitive health and antioxidant defenses. Unlike isolated chemical stimulants, this blend incorporates bioactive compounds that work together to support neurotransmitter function, protect neurons from oxidative damage, improve metabolic health, and promote fat loss.

Important Safety Information: This formula contains caffeine and thermogenic agents (Grains of Paradise). Individuals with cardiovascular conditions, high blood pressure, anxiety disorders, or those taking medications should consult their healthcare provider before use.
