Benefits

Raw Cacao Powder (7 g)
Rich in flavonoids (catechins and epicatechins) that improve blood flow, supporting cardiovascular and cognitive function. Contains theobromine (~84 mg) for gentle stimulation, mood enhancement, and vasodilation. Phenylethylamine (~35 mg) and anandamide (~140 μg) contribute to mood elevation and neurotransmitter balance, while also addressing deficiencies common in neurodegenerative conditions. Includes theophylline (~1.4 mg), which aids respiratory and smooth muscle relaxation.
Coconut Milk Powder (4 g)
Provides 3g medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) and saturated fat, enhancing absorption of fat-soluble compounds and supporting ketone production. Thes fat content optimizes the absorption of curcuminoids, carotenoids, and other bioactive compounds in the formula.
Dead Sea Salt (2 g)
Supplies a well-rounded electrolyte profile, to maintain hydration, nerve signaling, and muscle function.
Mushroom Blend (3 g)

Contains hericenones and erinacines that promote BDNF and nerve growth factor (NGF) synthesis, supporting neuronal health, memory, and cognitive performance.
Cordyceps sinensis Powder (1 g)

The polysaccharides provide additional adaptogenic benefits, supporting stress resilience and immune function.
Organic Ginger (1.2 g)

Ceylon Cinnamon (1 g)
Exhibits anti-hyperglycemic effects, improves blood glucose levels, and reduces inflammation. Lacks coumarin, a carcinogen found in other cinnamon types.
Myo-Inositol (750 mg)
Supports neuronal connectivity, neurotransmitter signaling, glucose metabolism, and hormonal balance. May enhance cognitive function and emotional well-being. Paquette, 2023 (PMCID: PMC10374161)

Additionally, these compounds activate serotonin receptors, which can help boost mood and reduce pain sensitivity, potentially leading to more enjoyable, motivated, and uninhibited workouts.

Stimulants

This blend offers a balanced blend of naturally occurring stimulants from cacao, matcha, and CoffeeBerry. These ingredients provide cognitive and metabolic benefits with better antioxidant effects and without the jitteriness or energy crashes typically associated with pure caffeine.

Cacao contains theobromine, phenylethylamine (PEA), and anandamide, which collectively improve mood, focus, and neurotransmitter balance. Cacao’s catechins and epicatechins help prolong the effects of these bioactive compounds while offering antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits.

Matcha is rich in polyphenols like epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG), along with L-theanine, which synergistically enhance cognitive focus, mitigates oxidative stress, and reduces inflammation. The antioxidants in matcha also protect against neurodegeneration and support metabolic health.

CoffeeBerry provides a gentle caffeine boost complemented by its polyphenols, which enhance cognitive health and antioxidant defenses. Unlike isolated chemical stimulants, this blend incorporates bioactive compounds that work together to support neurotransmitter function, protect neurons from oxidative damage, improve metabolic health, and promote fat loss.

Important Safety Information: This formula contains alpha-2 adrenergic antagonists (yohimbine and rauwolscine) and other stimulant compounds. While these are present in conservative amounts, individuals with cardiovascular conditions, high blood pressure, anxiety disorders, or those taking medications (especially MAOIs, blood pressure medications, or stimulants) should consult their healthcare provider before use. Do not combine with additional yohimbe-containing supplements.
