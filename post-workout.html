Benefits

This Post-Workout Mix aids in muscle recovery and supports overall health optimization. Whey protein provides cysteine, which enhances glutathione production to combat oxidative stress. BCAAs, particularly leucine, reduce brain inflammation by modulating excess glutamate, improve exercise recovery, and increase cognitive function in older adults. They also contribute significantly to the thermic effect of food, boosting metabolism and aiding weight management.

Whey Protein

Cold filtration produces whey protein with less denaturing that occurs with other processing forms, resulting in superior amino acid profiles, higher BV (biological value) and PDCAAS (protein digestibility corrected amino acid score) and improved microbial quality. This is the only form of whey used in this product. France, PMID: 34574201

HMB (β-hydroxy-β-methylbutyrate) enhances cell survival, tissue repair, and muscle stem cell differentiation. It increases muscle protein synthesis by stimulating the GH/IGF-1 axis and promotes muscle stem cell proliferation and differentiation, as shown in the study by Kaczka, PMID: 34574201.

Creatine Monohydrate supports energy metabolism by regenerating ATP in the ATP-PC system for rapid energy demands during high-intensity exercise. It reduces mitochondrial workload, scavenges free radicals, and minimizes cellular damage, promoting longevity of muscle, brain, and cardiac cells. Creatine also enhances muscular strength and recovery, complementing physical activity for optimal performance and health.

TMG (Betaine) regulates GABA and glycine, increases glutathione activity, and lowers homocysteine levels, while turmeric promotes neuroprotection, neurogenesis, and inflammation reduction. Ceylon Cinnamon enhances glucose uptake, glycogen synthesis, and GLP-1 levels, supporting metabolic health.

The Organic Berry Blend (acai, blueberry, elderberry, goji, and pomegranate) is rich in anthocyanins, polyphenols, and organic acids. These compounds provide potent antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective benefits. Goji berry, in particular, exhibits anti-diabetic properties, while the polyphenols and organic acids enhance gut health by promoting short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) production, nourishing beneficial bacteria, and supporting gut lining integrity. The synergistic effects of whole berries may outperform individual components, making the blend a valuable addition for post-exercise recovery and overall wellness. Venturi, PMCID: PMC10142833

Ginger improves blood-brain barrier function, stimulates mitophagy, and boosts circulation, protein absorption, and gastrointestinal function. Calcium AKG optimizes mitochondrial performance, and sodium bicarbonate buffers hydrogen ions, preventing pH drops in muscles and blood during intense exercise.
