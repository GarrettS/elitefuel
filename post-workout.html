Benefits

This Post-Workout Mix is formulated to optimize physical and cognitive recovery from exercise, supporting cellular and metabolic health. The formula combines generous dosages of proteins, amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and bioactive compounds that work synergistically to promote muscle protein synthesis, reduce oxidative stress, support mitochondrial function, and enhance nutrient absorption.

Cold-Filtered Whey Protein Isolate (48 g yielding 43 g protein)
Superior amino acid profile through cold filtration minimizes protein denaturation, resulting in higher biological value (BV) and protein digestibility corrected amino acid score (PDCAAS). Rich in cysteine, which enhances glutathione production - a crucial antioxidant for combating post-exercise oxidative stress. The 43g protein dosage exceeds the leucine threshold (2.5-3g), augmented by additional BCAA and HMB, needed to maximally stimulate muscle protein synthesis and prolong thermogenesis (as leucine is a ketogenic amino acid and cannot contribute to gluconeogenesis), particularly beneficial in the post-exercise window.
BCAA Blend (2 g, 4:1:1 Leucine:Isoleucine:Valine)
The enhanced leucine ratio (1.6g) complements whey protein's anabolic effects. Leucine activates mTOR signaling, while isoleucine enhances glucose uptake in muscle tissue. Valine competes with tryptophan for blood-brain barrier transport, potentially reducing central fatigue. This specific ratio optimizes the thermic effect of food, supporting metabolic rate elevation post-exercise.
Creatine Monohydrate (5 g)
Clinical dosage for maximizing muscle phosphocreatine stores. Supports rapid ATP regeneration during high-intensity exercise, reduces mitochondrial stress, and provides neuroprotective benefits through antioxidant properties. The 5g dose ensures optimal saturation of muscle stores while supporting cognitive function through improved brain energy metabolism.
HMB (β-hydroxy-β-methylbutyrate) (1 g)
This leucine metabolite enhances muscle protein synthesis while reducing protein breakdown. The 1g dose has been shown to improve recovery, particularly when combined with whey protein. HMB activates the mTOR pathway through a mechanism complementary to leucine, while also promoting muscle stem cell proliferation and differentiation.
Organic Berry Blend (15 g)
Comprehensive blend of berries providing diverse polyphenols, anthocyanins, and flavonoids. Each component offers unique benefits: Acai delivers high ORAC values and omega fatty acids; Blueberries provide neuroprotective pterostilbene; Elderberry offers immune support; Goji provides unique peptidoglycans and beta-carotene; Pomegranate enhances nitric oxide production. The polyphenols act as postbiotics, increasing short-chain fatty acid (SCFA) production in the gut, supporting microbiome health and intestinal barrier function. The 15g dose ensures therapeutic levels of bioactive compounds while supporting post-exercise inflammation reduction.
Electrolyte Blend (5 g)
The Post-Workout Electrolyte complex has less Ca and Na (which are excitatory) than Pre-Workout Base, to support rehydration and recovery:
  • Sodium Bicarbonate (1 g): Optimal dose for buffering exercise-induced acidosis
  • Potassium (719.5 mg total): Supports membrane potential and muscle contraction
  • Magnesium (205 mg total as bicarbonate and glycinate): Enhanced bioavailability forms supporting energy metabolism and muscle recovery
  • Calcium AKG (250 mg): Supports muscle contraction and bone health
Sodium-Potassium Pump

The sodium-potassium pump (Na+/K+-ATPase) maintains the electrochemical gradient across cell membranes, essential for nerve impulse propagation, muscle contraction, and cellular homeostasis.

Post-workout, the pump is active in re-establishing ion balance; thus, both sodium and potassium are important. However, potassium's role becomes more significant for recovery as it is essential for repolarization of the cell membrane, aiding in muscle relaxation and reducing fatigue, glycogen resynthesis, and muscle protein synthesis.

Fiber Blend (6 g - Inulin 5g, Glucomannan 1g)
Dual-action fiber complex supporting gut health and nutrient absorption. Inulin selectively feeds beneficial bacteria, promoting short-chain fatty acid production. Glucomannan's gel-forming properties slow gastric emptying, improving nutrient absorption and supporting blood glucose regulation.
Ashwagandha (KSM-66) (250 mg)
Standardized for withanolides, KSM-66 modulates the HPA axis, reducing cortisol and stress response, aiding post-exercise recovery. Its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anxiolytic effects, synergize with phosphatidylserine. Studies typically use 300 mg twice daily, showing it lowers anxiety, blood pressure, and increases DHEA-S and hemoglobin in stressed adults. It has been shown to increase testosterone in men and, in women, to improve hormonal cycle, menopause symptoms, fertility, and sexual health.
Bioactive Complex (1.6 g)
  • Turmeric (300 mg): Provides curcuminoids and turmerones, enhanced by the presence of phospholipids and coconut fat for improved absorption
  • Ginger (300 mg): Contains gingerols and shogaols, supporting circulation and digestion
  • Ceylon Cinnamon (700 mg): Supports glucose metabolism and contains unique polyphenols
  • Annatto Seed Powder (300 mg): Rich in bixin and norbixin carotenoids, specifically shown to protect against age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and cataracts. These compounds filter harmful blue light, protecting retinal health, and may improve night vision. While the 300 mg dose is conservative, chronic use, especially in combination with other carotenoids present in the NeuroFuel:Post add-on, supports long-term neural transmission and rod and cone function in macular health. The antioxidant profile helps protect against DNA damage and supports cellular health.
AKI Omega 3 DHA Powder (500 mg, yielding 100 mg DHA)
Stabilized DHA powder supporting cognitive function and reducing inflammation. The powder form ensures stability and improved bioavailability, while the 100 mg DHA contributes to daily omega-3 needs, particularly important for neural recovery.
Beta Alanine (2 g)
Precursor to carnosine, enhancing intramuscular buffering capacity. The 1g dose contributes to daily loading requirements for improving high-intensity exercise performance and reducing fatigue. Taking 4-6 g beta-alanine in divided doses over a month can significantly increase physical performance while reducing paraesthesia (tingling). (Trexler; 2015; PMCID: PMC4501114)
TMG (Betaine) (1,000 mg)
Supports methylation processes and osmolyte function at the clinically effective 1g dose. Enhances protein synthesis pathways while supporting liver function and homocysteine metabolism.
Vitamin and Mineral Complex
  • Vitamin B12 (Methylcobalamin, 100 mcg): Active form supporting energy metabolism and methylation
  • Folate (Methylfolate, 400 mcg): Bioavailable form supporting DNA synthesis and repair
  • Vitamin K2 (MK-7, 2 mg): Long-acting form supporting calcium utilization
  • P-5-P (10 mg): Active B6 supporting amino acid metabolism
  • Vitamin D3 (500 IU): Supporting hormone production and immune function
Phosphatidylserine (Sharp-PS) (150 mg)
Clinically validated form supporting cognitive function and cortisol regulation. The 150 mg dose has been shown to support exercise recovery while enhancing neural membrane function and neurotransmitter signaling. As a phospholipid, PS significantly improves the absorption and bioavailability of carotenoids (including bixin and norbixin from annatto), curcuminoids from turmeric, and other fat-soluble compounds in the formula through enhanced micelle formation and improved cellular uptake. This phospholipid-based delivery system is particularly crucial for optimizing the absorption of the formula's bioactive compounds.
Coconut Milk Powder (4 g)
Provides 3g medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) and saturated fat, enhancing absorption of fat-soluble compounds and supporting ketone production. The specific fat content optimizes the absorption of curcuminoids, carotenoids, and other bioactive compounds in the formula.
Astragalus (800 mg)
Adaptogenic herb supporting immune function and cardiovascular health. Contains unique polysaccharides and astragalosides that enhance mitochondrial function and cellular resilience, particularly beneficial during post-exercise recovery.
