Benefits

Whey Protein Isolate (48 g)
Provides high-quality, rapidly absorbed protein essential for muscle repair and growth. Its rich leucine content enhances the thermic effect of food (TEF) and stimulates muscle protein synthesis (MPS). Synergistic with BCAAs and HMB for optimal recovery.
Organic Berry Blend (Acai, Blueberry, Elderberry, Goji, Pomegranate)
Supplies potent antioxidants that combat exercise-induced oxidative stress. Acai and blueberry are rich in anthocyanins that support cognitive and cardiovascular health. Pomegranate enhances nitric oxide production, improving vascular function.
Fiber Blend (Inulin, Glucomannan, 6 g Dietary Fiber)
Promotes satiety and gut health. Inulin fosters beneficial gut bacteria, while glucomannan forms a viscous gel, slowing gastric emptying and stabilizing blood sugar. Supports nutrient absorption synergy with electrolytes and bioactives.
Electrolyte Blend
  • Sodium Bicarbonate (158 mg Sodium): Buffers lactic acid, reducing fatigue during high-intensity exercise.
  • Potassium Bicarbonate and Chloride (497 mg Potassium): Supports fluid balance, nerve transmission, and muscle contraction.
  • Magnesium Bicarbonate (52 mg Magnesium): Enhances energy metabolism and muscle relaxation.
  • Calcium AKG (75 mg Calcium): Facilitates muscle contraction and recovery, with AKG promoting mitochondrial health.
BCAA Blend (Leucine, Isoleucine, Valine in a 4:1:1 Ratio, 2 g)
Leucine drives MPS, amplified by whey protein synergy. Isoleucine aids glucose uptake in muscle, while valine reduces fatigue by competing with tryptophan transport to the brain.
Creatine Monohydrate (5 g)
Increases ATP availability, enhancing strength and power output. Supports lean muscle mass maintenance and reduces exercise-induced muscle damage.
HMB (1 g)
Protects muscle tissue by reducing protein breakdown. Enhances recovery and synergizes with leucine for MPS, particularly in calorie-restricted conditions.
AKI Omega 3 DHA Powder (0.5 g, 100 mg DHA)
DHA supports cognitive health and reduces inflammation. Works synergistically with turmeric and astragalus for optimal post-exercise recovery and brain function.
Bioactive Blend (Turmeric, Ginger, Ceylon Cinnamon)
  • Turmeric (300 mg): Contains curcumin, reducing inflammation via NF-κB modulation.
  • Ginger (300 mg): Enhances antioxidant defenses and supports joint health.
  • Ceylon Cinnamon (500 mg): Aids glucose metabolism and provides antimicrobial benefits.
These compounds work together to mitigate oxidative damage and support systemic recovery.
Astragalus (800 mg)
Boosts immune function and enhances mitochondrial function, promoting cellular resilience during recovery.
TMG (Betaine, 1 g)
Supports methylation processes critical for homocysteine reduction and DNA repair. May enhance endurance through improved oxygen delivery.
Beta Alanine (1 g)
Increases muscle carnosine levels, buffering hydrogen ions and delaying muscular fatigue during anaerobic exercise.
Vitamins
  • Vitamin D3 (~1000 IU): Enhances calcium absorption, supporting bone health and muscle function.
  • Vitamin K2 (MK-7, 2 mg): Works with D3 for optimal bone health and vascular integrity.
  • Vitamin B12 (Methylcobalamin, ~100 mcg): Facilitates energy production and red blood cell formation.
  • Folate (Methylfolate, 400 mcg): Supports DNA synthesis and repair, synergizing with B12 for methylation.
  • P-5-P (10 mg): Active form of B6, crucial for amino acid metabolism and neurotransmitter synthesis.

Cold filtration produces whey protein with less denaturing that occurs with other processing forms, resulting in superior amino acid profiles, higher BV (biological value) and PDCAAS (protein digestibility corrected amino acid score) and improved microbial quality. This is the only form of whey used in this product. France, PMID: 34574201

The synergistic effects of whole berries may outperform individual components, making the blend a valuable addition for post-exercise recovery and overall wellness.
