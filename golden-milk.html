Nighttime Golden Milk

A ritual for restful sleep and recovery.

Indulge in a soothing blend of rich spices and functional botanicals designed to promote restful sleep and recovery. Featuring creamy notes of turmeric, saffron, cinnamon, and ginger, this golden elixir is enhanced with adaptogens, medicinal mushrooms, and collagen for a comforting and nourishing nighttime ritual. A warm, aromatic escape that helps you unwind and wake up refreshed.

Price per serving: $4

Instructions

  1. Boil 4oz of water.
  2. Add 1 tsp of coconut oil, butter, or ghee (optional).
  3. Add 14oz whole milk and stir thoroughly.
  4. Stir the powder to hot (not boiling) liquid.

Ingredients

Collagen Peptides, Coconut Sugar/Date Sugar, Turkey Tail, Tremella, Reishi, Chaga, Lion’s Mane, Turmeric, Ceylon Cinnamon, Ginger Powder, Cardamom Powder, Saffron, Ashwagandha, Astragalus, Long Pepper, Clove Powder, Nutmeg, L-Theanine.

Collagen Peptides (7g)
Essential for connective tissue repair, supports skin elasticity, strengthens joints, and aids muscle recovery. Collagen also enhances gut health by fortifying the intestinal lining.
Turmeric (2g)
Curcumin offers strong anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, protecting against oxidative damage, supporting joint mobility, brain health, and cardiovascular function.
Turkey Tail (1g)
Rich in polysaccharides that support immune system modulation, improve gut microbiota diversity, and exhibit potential antitumor effects.
Ceylon Cinnamon (1.2g)
Supports blood sugar regulation, enhances insulin sensitivity, and provides antimicrobial properties while reducing oxidative stress.
Annatto Seed Powder (500 mg)
Rich in bixin and norbixin carotenoids, which protect against age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and cataracts. These compounds filter harmful blue light, protecting retinal health, and may improve night vision. While the 500 mg dose is conservative, chronic use, especially in combination with other carotenoids present in this formula, supports long-term neural transmission and rod and cone function in macular health. The antioxidant profile helps protect against DNA damage and supports cellular health.
Ginger Powder (1.2g)
Contains gingerol, which improves digestion, reduces nausea, and provides anti-inflammatory and antioxidant benefits.
Tremella (1g)
Boosts hydration by enhancing hyaluronic acid synthesis, promotes skin elasticity, and offers antioxidant protection against cellular damage.
Reishi (1g)
Supports immune balance, reduces fatigue, and promotes calmness via interaction with GABAergic pathways, improving sleep quality and stress resilience.
Chaga (1g)
Rich in melanin and betulinic acid, Chaga strengthens the immune system, protects against oxidative damage, and enhances skin health.
Lion’s Mane (1g)
Stimulates nerve growth factor (NGF), promoting neuronal repair, enhancing synaptic plasticity, and potentially improving memory and cognition.
Coconut Sugar/Date Sugar (1g)
Natural sweeteners providing trace minerals, including potassium and magnesium, supporting enzymatic reactions and maintaining electrolyte balance.
Cardamom Powder (0.8g)
Improves digestion by stimulating bile secretion, offers antimicrobial activity, and supports respiratory health.
Long Pepper (0.5g)
Increases nutrient bioavailability, supports digestive health, and provides anti-inflammatory effects via piperlongumine.
Clove Powder (0.4g)
Rich in eugenol, which offers powerful antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory properties, supporting oral health and reducing oxidative stress.
Saffron (70mg)
Contains crocin and safranal, which provide neuroprotective, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects. Clinically shown to improve mood, enhance sleep quality, and protect visual function. Demonstrated benefits for neurodegenerative diseases through modulation of neuroinflammation and oxidative stress pathways.
Ashwagandha (0.3g)
Adaptogen that lowers cortisol levels, enhances stress resilience, supports thyroid function, and improves muscle recovery and stamina.
Astragalus (0.2g)
Supports immune response with saponins and flavonoids, reduces inflammation, and enhances cardiovascular health by improving endothelial function.
L-Theanine (50–100mg)
Promotes relaxation and improves sleep by enhancing GABA, dopamine, and serotonin production while inhibiting stress hormones.
Nutmeg (Pinch)
Contains myristicin and macelignan, supporting digestion, promoting relaxation, and exhibiting mild sedative properties.
