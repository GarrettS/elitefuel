Pre-Workout Base

Cellular energy, mental focus, hydration, and electrolytes for peak performance and endurance.

Acute Effects

Within 30 minutes, you’ll notice improved focus, enhanced stamina, and reduced fatigue, preparing you for a high-intensity session. Hydration and blood flow are optimized, so you’re ready to push harder and recover faster.

Chronic Effects

With consistent, regular use over several months, daily workouts become more productive, recovery times shorten, and endurance and strength improve, allowing for more intense and frequent training. The cumulative effects enhance both performance and long-term adaptation to training demands. Cognitive function may improve slightly due to the nootropic ingredients. Illness-related setbacks may become less frequent. Over time, the cumulative effects of more intense training may contribute to better sleep quality, aiding in recovery and overall stress resilience. These benefits combine for productive training and improved overall fitness.

Hydration

Hydration is critical to facilitate the transport and absorption of the concentrated ingredients throughout the body and brain, support cellular hydration and volumization, maintain fluid balance to prevent cramping during intense activity, and aid digestion. Consume at least 16–32 oz (500–1000 mL) of water with each serving of Pre-Workout Base, plus 250 mL for each add-on. Stay hydrated throughout your workout to maximize ingredient performance and avoid dizziness or discomfort.

Recommendation: For optimal results, consume the Cacao Mix at least 15 minutes before taking the Pre-Workout. This pre-hydration step optimizes electrolyte levels and gastric emptying, enhancing the digestion and absorption of the Base. The Cacao Mix also provides naturally occurring stimulants that synergize with the Base's lipolytic ingredients, sharpening mental focus and promoting fat loss.

Calcium Balance

Calcium balance is delicate: excess can lead to intracellular overload (via IP3R pathways), causing calcium transport from the ER to mitochondria, while insufficient pre-workout calcium can triggers bone resorption. The moderate amount of calcium, D3, and K2 in Pre-Workout Base, along with exercise helps retain bone calcium exercise performance.

Enhanced NAD+ Support

CellCharge provides high doses of NAD+ precursors (NMN, NR, Niacin), which dose-dependently:

Consider the combination, if optimal cognitive performance and cellular health are both priorities in your training regimen.
