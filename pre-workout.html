Should I add both CellCharge and NeuroDrive:Pre to my Pre-Workout Base?

While each add-on is effective on its own, adding CellCharge can increase the effects of NeuroDrive:Pre, particularly for those who are:

  • Have intensive cognitive or mental performance demands
  • Over 30 and focused on both performance and longevity
  • Want to maximize mental performance
  • Need to combat high physical and mental stress
  • Are looking for comprehensive cellular and cognitive support
  • Train intensely and need enhanced recovery support

CellCharge significantly amplifies NeuroDrive:Pre's cognitive benefits through multiple synergistic pathways:

Enhanced NAD+ Support

CellCharge provides high doses of NAD+ precursors (NMN, NR, Niacin), which dose-dependently:

  • Improve neurotransmission and synaptic plasticity
  • Enhance mitochondrial function in neuronal cells
  • Activate of protective sirtuins that reduce neuroinflammation
  • Prolong cognitive endurance and mental clarity

NeuroDrive:Pre optimizes cognitive function. CellCharge supports the cellular energy needed to sustain that heightened mental state. Together they provide comprehensive NAD+ support through multiple pathways. Both formulas work to enhance mitochondrial function, but through complementary mechanisms.

Consider the combination if optimal cognitive performance and cellular health are both priorities in your training regimen.
